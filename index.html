about me

My career started as a carpenter, learning from my dad right out of high school. Over the years my specialty became rough carpentry or creating the skeleton of a building. As an experienced carpenter and maintenance professional, I mastered a variety of tasks over the years, which has sharpened my ability to learn and improve processes, and team up successfully with a variety of personalities. As my family grew so did my ambition to develop and provide. In the spirit of challenging myself mentally and professionally, I decided to take my rough carpentry skills online. My goal is to now create the skeletons of websites with HTML, building them out with CSS/JS. Today, I am a student working to become a full-stack developer. I am looking to join a company that encourages new ideas, learning and career growth.

Currently I lead the worship team at church where I am reponsible for organizing service structure and leading the congregation in songs. This is where I can exercise my passion for singing and playing guitar. Often my weekends are spent watching the Chiefs and exploring downtown Kansas City with my wife.

Languages: HTML/CSS, JavaScript
