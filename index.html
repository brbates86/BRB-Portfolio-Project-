From carpenter to coder.


My career started as a carpenter, learning from my dad right out of high school. Over the years, my specialty became 'rough carpentry', or framing the skeleton of a project. As my family grew, so did my ambition to develop and provide. Thus began my journey to digitize my framing skills. I can confirm carpentry and coding are nothing alike outside of the words framing and skeleton. Today, I am a student at KU working to become a full-stack developer. My time is also spent leading the worship team at church where I exercise my passion for singing and playing guitar. My weekends often consist of watching the Chiefs and exploring downtown Kansas City with my wife, Claire My goal is to join a team that encourages new ideas, learning and career growth.
